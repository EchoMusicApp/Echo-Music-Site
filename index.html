Music

Playback, discovery, and listening experience.

Recommendations (Echo Brain)

Fully local intelligence with transparent recommendation logic.

  • Runs on-device with privacy-first design
  • Detects mood and genre preferences
  • Recommends forgotten favorites
  • Avoids repetitive recommendations
  • Learns from likes, skips, repeats, and listening time
  • Separates morning, afternoon, and night listening habits
  • Mixes familiar tracks with new discoveries
  • Export and import recommendation profiles
